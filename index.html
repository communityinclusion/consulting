Our Approach

We tailor our resources to ensure you fulfill your organizations particular vision of meeting its mission: supporting and empowering people with disabilities. We’re here to help you grow, thrive, and measure your progress.

How do we fuel organizations to exceed expectations?

Our approach is modeled after Kouzes and Posner’s Practices of Exemplary Leadership:1

  • Challenge current reality and identify objectives.
  • Innovate desired outcomes and plan development.
  • Empower and plan integration for change.
  • Encourage staying the course.
  • Assess outcomes to ensure continuous improvement.

Our consultation services help you:

Challenge the status quo. Together, we’ll look critically at your organizational vision, mission, philosophical underpinnings, policy, and practice. We’ll determine what’s hindering your progress toward your goals.

Innovate a plan for desired outcomes. With our expert insights, you’ll establish how you want things to look and be, determining the best actions and next steps. What needs to happen for you to reach the culture you envision?

Empower to carry out the plan. Through participatory leadership, you’ll guide the change process, delegating to others, and allowing for innovation and problem solving at all levels. We’ll also prepare you to honor the new vision and values, and spread the message about them.

Encourage full engagement. We’ll show you how to involve all staff members in staying the course and keeping the plan a priority. This includes building communication feedback loops, reinforcing efforts, and celebrating successes.

Assess results. We’ll work with you to track meaningful elements and peripheral impacts to compare to desired outcomes. We’ll conduct a 360° evaluation, considering both quantitative and qualitative factors. This leads to your next challenge and even higher achievements.
